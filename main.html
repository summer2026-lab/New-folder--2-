My favourite sports and pass time is cricket

Cricket is a game which consists of batting and bowling

The batsman faces 6 deliveries from the bowler this is called an over.The batsman must try and score as many runs as possible.

The bowler

The bowler has 6 deliveries to bowl to the batsman and try his best to get him out and get hit for the least amount of runs

The umpire

The job of the umpire is to make the game fair and judge decisions of the game such as LBW or no ball or wides.

The commentator

The commentator's job is to keep the audience entertained and explained throughout.

The fielders

The fielders job is to save the bowler being hit runs and to throw the ball back to the bowler or wicketkeeper they play a key role in the game
